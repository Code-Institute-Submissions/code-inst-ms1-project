Kinesiotaping

Kinesiotaping is dynamic slicing, it involves sticking various parts of the body with special colored tapes to relieve pain and also to prevent injuries.

Therpay duration: 30 min

Scar therapy

Scar therapy is introduced in post-operative rehabilitation to reduce the visibility of the scar, as well as improve functionality (reduce the feeling of pulling). more...

The scarring is caused by injuries or various types of surgical procedures in which the continuity of the skin and deeper tissues is broken. It cannot be prevented, but you can affect its appearance and proper healing, which will reduce the risk of later complications.

Therpay duration: 30 min

Trigger needle therapy

Needle therapy is one of the most effective forms of trigger point therapy. It aims to locate the trigger point of a given muscle, followed by a needle puncture in this area, which will cause local muscle contraction. Thanks to this procedure, the muscle relaxes and the symptoms disappear.

Therpay duration: 30 min

Sports massage

It is a form of bodywork geared toward participants in athletics. It is used to help prevent injuries, to prepare the body for athletic activity and maintain it in optimal condition, and to help athletes recover from workouts and injuries.

Therpay duration: 60 min

Relaxing massage

Smooth, gentle, flowing style that promotes general relaxation, relieves muscular tension, plus improves circulation and range of movement. This is the style of massage you'll find performed at day spas.

Therpay duration: 60 min

Massage

Treatment which aims to relax, revive and heal the body. Using manual hands-on movements and manipulation of the soft tissues and muscles in the body, massage therapy works to promote healing and enhance a person's overall well-being.

Therpay duration: 30 min

Lymphatic drainage

It is kind of a therapeutic massage treatment. The massage uses very light pressure and long, gentle, rhythmic strokes to increase the flow of lymph and reduce toxins in your body.

Therpay duration: from 30 to 60 min

Manual therapy

Physical treatment treat musculoskeletal pain and disability; it most includes kneading and manipulation of muscles, joint mobilization and joint manipulation.
